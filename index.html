About

GDPR compliant data sharing

Thanks to the Open Neuro PET project, we have built a GDPR compliant repository to share brain imaging data as FAIRly as possibly. The principle is similar to OpenNeuro where each dataset is shared independently. As EU data have to be protected, we provide the necessary privacy protection.
